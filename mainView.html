About Me.

I enjoy creating - whether that’s using Java, HTML, JavaScript, Objective-C, Python, InDesign, Premiere, Final Cut, pen or pencil. My greatest inspirations in life are Ferris Bueller and, of course, my family. In my spare time I like to dance, run and read.
